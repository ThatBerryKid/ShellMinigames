Bunker Wars is a minigame where players keep the spatula in the bunker the entire match while trying to get the most points before time runs out!

  • HOW TO PLAY
  • CREATOR TIPS
  • PLAYER TIPS

How to play:
  1. Select the Capture The Spatula gamemode.
  2. Get the spatula and put it in the bunker of the map.
  3. Set a score you want the team to get to, and how long the match will be.
  4. Boot anyone who takes the spatula outside of the bunker.
  5. Whichever team gets their points to the limit first or has the higher points when the timer ends, is the winner!
  6. Remember to have fun!

Tips for Content Creators:

  • Here are some suggested maps to play on:

    Dirt Base

    Jail Break

    Mud Gulch

    Road

    Shipyard

    Sparta

    Stage

  • If you play this on Twitch, having a Channel Points Prediction for the viewers to participate in will add some fun!

  • 7 minutes is the recommended time limit for each round of Bunker Wars. (9 minutes is a good amount as well, and 12 minutes if you're feeling comfortable or that the rounds are going too quickly)

  • If you have a timer, displaying it as a timer overlay on screen is helpful for the viewers to keep track of close finishes! (in Twitch clips, or rewinding on YouTube)

  • Kick out those who don't listen. The game only works if people listen! They can always join back in the next rounds if they want to play along!

  • Try to get an even number for the teams (ex 2v2, 4v4, 8v8 - including you). It's best to not start until the teams are balanced. But if you just can't get enough people to balance the teams out, it's fine, I've been there too.

  • If you plan to watch the action happen the whole time, or record a Bunker Wars event between your viewers/players, join spectator mode and make sure the teams are an even number without you.

  • A cool idea for an event/YouTube video is hosting a 4v4 Bunker Wars with 2 or more spectators recording other point-of-view shots

  • Have fun! If it feels like it will be too much work for your viewers instead of play,
    try a different minigame to play!
