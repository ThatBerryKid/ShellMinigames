In Bunker Wars, the players keep the spatula in the bunker and try to get their team's points to the goal before time runs out!

HOW TO PLAY | CREATOR TIPS | PLAYER

How to play:
  1. Select the Capture The Spatula gamemode.
  2. Get the spatula and put it in the bunker of the map.
  3. Set a score you want the team to get to, and how long the match will be.
  4. Boot anyone who takes the spatula outside of the bunker.
  5. Whichever team gets to the goal in points first or has the higher points when the timer ends, is the winner!
  6. Remember to have fun!
